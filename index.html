✨ Pam Selle ✨

Pam Selle (pronounced "Sell-EE") is a writer, musician, technologist, and dancer. This is her homepage on the internet from whence you may (possibly) find other things she's up to.

Join my newsletter for infrequent updates and ephemera if you like.

Ongoing Projects

Things I'm doing that are updated fairly regularly.

  • Recording as host of Ignore All Previous Instructions, a podcast about tech and its intersections with culture and society. Watch on YouTube, or listen through other streaming platforms.
  • Dance teaching at my private practice.
  • Blogging at thewebivore.com: writing about technology and everything else as well.
  • Music at beats.thewebivore.com, for irregularly released tracks, and I'm also 50% of CatFans [the band].

Projects

Things I've made that are encapsulated (not ongoing).

  • Music/Film: Project Brain States (2020), created with a team of 3 collaborating over 5 days via gmail and featuring my music.
  • Web/Art: The Postcard Wall (2020).

  • Zine: Serverless zine (2018).
  • Podcast: Turing-Incomplete, which recorded 100 episodes before stopping in 2017.
  • Book: Beyond the Resume (2016), an opinionated guide on getting your next developer job by leveraging your network.

Community Work

Communities I facilitate or cat herd, both now and in the past.

  • Remote in Philly: Launched in 2019, to form a conversation around people who work remotely but live in the Philadelphia region. Inactive.
  • PhillyDev Slack: I served admin team of this large online community of developers in/around Philadelphia for many years.
  • Conference: Cofounded LibertyJS, Philadelphia's JavaScript conference, which has been running for 5+ years. I keynoted in 2017. (no longer actively involved)
  • Meetups: I was an organizer for the Philadelphia JavaScript Developers meetup for three years, going from a dozen to over 1k members. I helped with the the Serverless meetup for a year+.
