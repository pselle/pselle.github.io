✨ Dance with Pam ✨

I've been teaching partner dance since 2021 in Philadelphia, and teach out of a private studio located in East Passyunk.

I particularly love helping people who are new to dance and helping them connect to the music, their partner, and themselves :) Dancing is such a rewarding activity, a challenging skill, and something to work on no matter what age or ability.

My students are both singles and couples; I love helping leaders gain confidence and couples taking time to connect with each other.

Some of the dances I teach:

  • Argentine Tango
  • Swing (East and West Coast)
  • Salsa
  • Bachata
  • American Ballroom: Waltz, Foxtrot, Tango, Rumba …

Not sure where to start? No problem! I love introducing folks to dance and we discover where your passions lie!
